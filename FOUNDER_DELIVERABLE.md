## What We Built

### **Core Product: AI-Powered Real Estate Transaction Assistant**

The prototype showcases **ARIA** (Automated Real Estate Intelligence Assistant), an AI system that automates the entire property-to-contract workflow. The demo uses real San Diego property data to demonstrate authentic, professional-grade automation capabilities.

### **Key Features Demonstrated:**

1. **AI Property Discovery**: Interactive chat interface showing ARIA understanding client needs and finding perfect property matches
2. **Instant Property Analysis**: Automated investment metrics, comparable sales data, and market intelligence
3. **Document Generation**: Automated creation of 12+ legal documents including RPA, disclosures, and compliance forms
4. **Signature Workflow**: Orchestrated multi-party document signing with automated reminders and tracking
5. **Neighborhood Intelligence**: Comprehensive lifestyle and market analytics dashboard
